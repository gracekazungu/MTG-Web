MOVING THE GOALPOSTS

  • The Goalposts is a community based organisation that uses football to empower girls in Kenyan Coastal Counties of Kilifi, Kwale,Mombasa and TanaRiver.
  • It started in the year 2002 to harness the power to overcome the social obstacles that girls and young women face.
  • Long time ago girls and young women were looked down upon that they were experiencing problems like; poor access to Education,Early Marriage and school drop out.
  • MTG came up with programmes designed in such away that girls were taught on their Health, and even paid school fees to girls who were unable to continue with their Education.
  • Moving The Goalposts is the only organisation that links Gender-Based Violence with Reproductive Health
  • MTG works with girls at the age of 9-25years. Now has managed to work with over 9000 girls within the 4 Counties.
  • For one to become a member, she needs to undergo the registration process,one has to pay 5 shillings for one to be given the registration form.
  • One has to register to a nearby field where she will manage to go every Suturday For the Football Practices.

Health

In this department girls are having sessions on Mental Health and Reproductive health sessions.For the adolenscent girls, they are passed through sessions on menstral hygiene. For the girls between 18-25, their sessions are mainly on Family Planning,Mental Health. For the Young mothers they are always given information on the importance of ANC(Antenatal Care).

Child protection policy

This department deals with girls rights. It has rools that governs the girls and staffs in day to day life. It also teaches girls on ways to follow when incase their rigths are violated by maybe the staffs or anybody around them and they report cases regarding to child abuse in relevant authorities. They also take girls through sessions on Safeguarding.

Education and Livelihood

In Education, it deals on how the organisation helps some girls who have financial problems by paying hteir school fees inorder to continue with their Education. The Livelihooddepartment it deals with the girls who are out of school, maybe they completed Secondary,Primary,College or even dropped out of school.They helps the girls who want to join colleges by helping them join and also encourage them to start their business.

Leadership

In this Department, girls are given the chance to be trained some of leadership positions i.e Coaches,Referees, First Aiders, Field Leaders. Safeguarders are trained because of the Child protection department while Peer Educators are trainde because of the health deapartment

Finance
